# Available Commands

```/spawn```   <br>
```/sethome "name"``` (Save current location as home, maximum 3 homes)   <br>
```/home "name"``` (Teleport to the selected home)   <br>
```/tprandrom``` (Teleport to a random location)   <br>
```/tpa "Player"``` (Ask to teleport to a player)   <br>
```/tphere "Player"``` (Ask another player to teleport to you)   <br>
```/tpaccept``` (Accept another player's teleport request)   <br>
```/tpdeny``` (Deny another player's teleport request)   <br>
```/pvp [on|off]```   <br>

Use a golden shovel and right click to claim area   <br>

```/trust "player"``` (Gives a friend permission to build in your claim)   <br>
```/containertrust "player"``` (Allows a player to open chests/furnaces but not build)   <br>
```/accesstrust "player"``` (Allows a player to use buttons and levers only)   <br>
```/untrust "player"``` (Revokes trust)   <br>
```/abandonclaim``` (Deletes the claim you are currently standing in)   <br>
```/claimslist``` (Shows how many claim blocks you have left)   <br>
