# Available Commands

/spawn
/sethome "name" (Save current location as home, maximum 3 homes)
/home "name" (Teleport to the selected home)
/tprandrom (Teleport to a random location)
/tpa "Player" (Ask to teleport to a player)
/tphere "Player" (Ask another player to teleport to you)
/tpaccept (Accept another player's teleport request)
/tpdeny (Deny another player's teleport request)
/pvp [on|off]

Use a golden shovel and right click to claim area

/trust "player" (Gives a friend permission to build in your claim)
/containertrust "player" (Allows a player to open chests/furnaces but not build)
/accesstrust "player" (Allows a player to use buttons and levers only)
/untrust "player" (Revokes trust)
/abandonclaim (Deletes the claim you are currently standing in)
/claimslist (Shows how many claim blocks you have left)

/discord
/discord link
